## **Anadigm Software Setup**

- Open AnadigmDesigner2
- By default you will have the following working space

<img src="img/2_as2_default_working_space.png" width="60%">

!!! note
	For this test you can use any pin that you want.
 
- I will be using pin 01 and 02 (differential) as the input signal
- And pin 15 and 16 (differential) as the output signal
- Pin 01 corresponds to I1P and I1N

## **AD2 Setup**

- Open the function generator and enable a waveform of your choice.

!!! warning
	Make sure that your waveform is between 0 to 3.3V otherwise you can damage the FPAA board.


<img src="img/2_ad2_waveform_specs.png" width="80%">

- Open the scope and observe if the input
- Orange Line: input waveform connected to pin I1P (input 1 positive)
- Blue Line: output waveform connected to pin IO5P (input output 5 positive)

<img src="img/2_ad2_waveform_scope.png" width="100%">
